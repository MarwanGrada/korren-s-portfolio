about me

Korren is a visual communications professional, driven by her passion for design and illustration. Currently working for one of the country's largest integrated health systems, Northwell Health, she has helped create a new look for internal communications and various initiatives across all platforms for a regional workforce of more than 60,000 employees. She utilizes her skills to create consistent visual collateral, intended to either encourage employee engagement or inform external viewers about the health system's offerings. Projects include the Careers social media platform, leadership initiatives, internal branding and website design, event collateral, and external marketing materials.
